Strategic Concept

From the Cell
to the Street

A contraband phone inside a facility is not a security incident. It is a live line into the criminal network operating outside the wall.

Facilities seize thousands of contraband phones, but seizure is where most investigations stop. LeadsOnline turns the phone into intelligence. CellHawk works the call records to map who the inmate is running with, and NightHawk draws the inside-to-outside network: the suppliers, enablers, and associates on the street. This concept positions LeadsOnline as the intelligence layer that connects what happens inside the wall to the people directing crime beyond it, for the Bureau of Prisons, state corrections, and the task forces they feed.
